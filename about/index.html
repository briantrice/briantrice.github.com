About me

I'm a entrepreneur, programmer, teacher, mentor, speaker and author who spends most of my time hacking on open source and lecturing and speaking at developer conferences world-wide. I have worked at Terracotta, the JRockit JVM at BEA and is an active contributor to the Open Source community; most notably created the Akka Project, AspectWerkz Aspect-Oriented Programming (AOP) framework, committer to the Terracotta JVM clustering technology and been part of the Eclipse AspectJ team. In 2011 I also became a Java Champion.
